Our Mission

At VEX.blue, we are committed to bringing your stories to life with creativity, precision, and a personal touch. Founded by Samantha Xavia, a proud trans woman, our company embraces diversity and inclusion, providing a welcoming space for LGBT professionals to thrive.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional video editing services, preserve cherished memories through VHS conversion, and amplify voices with our podcast production. We believe in the power of storytelling to inspire, connect, and make a difference, and we are dedicated to making high-quality media accessible to everyone.
At VEX.blue, your vision is our passion. Together, let's create, preserve, and share your unique stories with the world.
